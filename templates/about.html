My Story

Early on I discovered that data isn't just numbers, it's the heartbeat of modern business. I dove deep into ETL pipelines and real-time analytics, then expanded into LLM agents, RAG systems, and cloud architectures to deliver end-to-end solutions. Today, I love architecting systems that run reliably at scale and uncovering insights that drive real impact.

Whether I'm building a RAG pipeline over clinical or regulatory documents, training a computer vision model for MRI classification, or automating deployments with Terraform and Kubernetes, my goal is the same: empower teams with robust, maintainable AI and data platforms.
