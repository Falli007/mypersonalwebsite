About Me

Who I Am

I am Fredrick Alli, a passionate Data Consultant specialising in providing data-driven solutions to various companies. With a background in machine learning, data engineering, and BI tools, I help businesses unlock actionable insights and drive better decisions.

My Background

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Science, MSc Data Science and Artificial Intelligence from the Southampton Solent University and have completed several certifications in data analytics, Power BI and business intelligence. My journey into data analysis began during my university years, where I discovered the power of data in solving real-world problems.

My Hobbies

When I am not crunching numbers or building dashboards, you can find me exploring the great outdoors, playing football, swimming, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. I believe in the importance of work-life balance and enjoy spending time with family and friends.

My Story

My passion for data analysis stems from my curiosity to understand the world around me. I am fascinated by the stories that data can tell and the impact it can have on businesses and society. I am committed to continuous learning and growth, always seeking new challenges and opportunities to expand my skill set.
